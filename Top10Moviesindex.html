My Top 10 Favorite Movies

1. My Sassy Girl

A romantic comedy about a man and a quirky girl.

2. Parasite

A dark thriller about social class struggles.

3. Extreme Job

A group of detectives goes undercover in a fried chicken restaurant.

4. I Saw The Devil

A brutal revenge thriller between a detective and a killer.

5. Midnight Runners

Two police trainees witness a kidnapping and take action.

6. The Dark Knight

Batman faces the Joker, who plunges Gotham into chaos.

7. Train to Busan

A zombie outbreak occurs on a moving train.

8. Dune: Part Two

Paul Atreides joins the Fremen to avenge his family and fulfill his destiny.

9. Hacksaw Ridge

The true story of Desmond Doss, a medic who saved soldiers in WWII without firing a gun.

10. 1917

A World War I film following two British soldiers on a dangerous mission to deliver a message that could save hundreds of lives.
